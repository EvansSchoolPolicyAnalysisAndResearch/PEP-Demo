Problem Statement

Too few Americans have access to equitable post-secondary education. With the Policy Exploration Platform, you can explore evidence from federal data on community colleges.

Purpose

This app allows you to explore the current landscape of post-secondary education opportunities and challenges in rural regions of the United States, focusing on community colleges. Using recent data from IPEDS, you can examine enrollment patterns, access to distance education, and demographic trends to better understand how rural students engage with higher education and what barriers they may face.
